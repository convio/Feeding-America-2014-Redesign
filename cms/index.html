How We're Ending Hunger.

Your gift is the critical first step in feeding millions of Americans at risk of hunger.

We build partnerships with

  • Growers
  • Processors
  • Retailers
  • Manufacturers
  • Wholesalers

Our network moves the food making sure donated food and groceries go where they are needed most.

Network food banks ensure safe storage and distribute the groceries to local charities.
